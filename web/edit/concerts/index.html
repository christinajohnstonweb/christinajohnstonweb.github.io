Display options

You can choose whether a concert is visible on the website, and whether it is featured on the homepage. Concerts are published by default when created, and visible in the Concerts tab. To change whether a concert is displayed on the site, you can turn publishing on and off with the toggle here. By default, concerts are not displayed on the homepage. To display them on the homepage, make sure they are published, and turn on the homepage toggle here.
